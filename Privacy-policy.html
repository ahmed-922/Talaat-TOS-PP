Data Retention
We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal requirements.
Account data: While account is active plus 2 years after deletion
Location data: Aggregated after 6 months, deleted after 2 years
Commercial data: Up to 7 years for financial compliance requirements
User-generated content: Until you request deletion
Analytics data: Anonymized and aggregated after 1 year
Support communications: 3 years for quality assurance
You can request earlier deletion of your data, subject to legal and safety requirements.
