Account Deletion Request - Talaat

Important: Before requesting account deletion, please note that this action is irreversible. All your data will be permanently deleted from our systems.

How to Request Account Deletion

Step 1: Open the Talaat app on your device
Step 2: Go to your Profile section
Step 3: Tap on "Settings" or "Account Settings"
Step 4: Scroll down and find "Delete Account" option
Step 5: Follow the confirmation prompts to complete the deletion

Alternative Method: Email Request

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion via email:

Email: privacy@talaat.com
Subject: Account Deletion Request
Include in your email:
  • Your registered email address
  • Your username (if different from email)
  • Reason for deletion (optional)
  • Confirmation that you understand this action is irreversible

Data Type Reason Retention Period

Partial Data Deletion

You can also delete specific content without deleting your entire account:

  • Individual Posts: Tap the three dots menu on any post and select "Delete"
  • Photos: Remove photos from your posts or profile
  • Reviews: Edit or delete your reviews for places
  • Profile Information: Update or remove specific profile details

After Deletion

  • Your account will be immediately deactivated
  • All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days
  • You will no longer be able to access the app with your credentials
  • You can create a new account at any time with a different email address
